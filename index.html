About Mountain Creek

Mountain Creek in New Jersey has a history that includes ski resorts, water parks, and other attractions.The water park was originally known as Action Park, and was one of the first modern American water parks. It opened in 1978, but was infamous for its safety record, with at least six deaths and many injuries. The park closed in 1995 after years of lawsuits and safety violations. It reopened in 1998 as Mountain Creek Waterpark after a major overhaul. In 2014, the name was changed back to Action Park, and then reverted to Mountain Creek Waterpark again a few years later.
